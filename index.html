About Us

Kala Drycleaners is a family-run dry cleaning business proudly serving Akola, Maharashtra for over 35 years. Founded and run by Gopal Chakar, we have built our reputation on quality craftsmanship, genuine care for every garment, and unwavering customer trust. From everyday laundry to delicate wedding outfits, we treat every piece as if it were our own.
